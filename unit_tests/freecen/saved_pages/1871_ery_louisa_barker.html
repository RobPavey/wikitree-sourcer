Donate

While most other genealogy sites charge you to find and read records, FreeCEN is dedicated to keeping our searches free. A small donation towards our costs would really help us keep it that way.

The FreeCEN database is just a finding tool. Whilst we make every effort to ensure accurate transcription, errors exist in both the original census and the transcription. Please verify any result with the actual census at your local library or similar resource.

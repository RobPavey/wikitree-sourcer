Search the GRO Online Indexes
 
Which index would you like to search?
Events registered in England or Wales:
When was the birth registered?
Year:* Mandatory field. Select year of registration rather than the year of the event if they differ.

GRO birth indexes are available for records from 1837 up to 1934 and from 1984 up to the current year. GRO death indexes are available for records from 1837 up to 1957 and 1984 up to the current year.

GRO is currently identifying a rolling programme to put more of its indexes online over time. We will notify you via our website when any new indexes are added.

You can view GRO's birth, death and marriage indexes online via other websites, e.g. FreeBMD, where they are available to view free of charge up to 1983. 		+/-  year(s) Search defaults to exact year entered but optional "+/-" can extend your search a further two years either side of the event.

Please enter your search criteria, mandatory fields are indicated with a *
Up to 250 matching results will be returned so providing any additional information you know will help locate the registration you are looking for.

Who are you searching for?
Surname at Birth:* Mandatory field. Surname of the person named in the entry, at the time the event was registered.   Include: Search defaults to Exact Matches but Phonetically/Similar Sounding variations available.
Search for records with a matching surname
First Forename: Non-mandatory field. Forename(s) of the person named in the entry, at the time the event was registered.   Include: Search defaults to Exact Matches but Phonetically/Similar Sounding variations available, with additional Derivative Name variation. e.g. "Peggy" for "Margaret", or "Betty" for "Elizabeth".
Search for records with a matching first forename
Second Forename: Non-mandatory field. You can enter second forename to narrow down search results.  
Sex:* Mandatory field. Male or Female must be selected. If the system holds this information it will use it to narrow down the search.  
Mother's Maiden Surname: Non-mandatory field. This may narrow down search results.
For modern birth records (between 0 - 100 years), the index return will only include Mother's Maiden Surname if you enter it in here correctly. If you leave this field blank, enter the wrong name or spell it incorrectly it will not be returned in the index. 		  Include: Search defaults to Exact Matches but Phonetically/Similar Sounding variations available.
Search for records with a matching Mother's Maiden Surname

Where was the birth registered?
District of Birth: Non-mandatory field. You can enter the district name to narrow down search results.

Do you already know any GRO Reference details for the registration?
Quarter: Non-mandatory Field. Records are submitted to the Registrar General quarterly.
   M=January - March;
   J=April - June;
   S=July - September;
   D=October - December
Can be used to narrow down search results.
Volume: Non-mandatory field. If you know the volume of the GRO index reference, this may narrow down search results. Page: Non-mandatory Field. If you know the Page Number of the GRO index reference, this may narrow down search results.
 

   
 

Results: Search results are returned in Surname, Forename, Secondary Forename, Year then Quarter order.
   M=Jan - Mar,
   J=Apr - Jun,
   S=Jul - Sep,
   D=Oct - Dec
Volume, page number, district and mother's maiden name are also shown.

Select the button by an individual result to see ordering options available for that record.

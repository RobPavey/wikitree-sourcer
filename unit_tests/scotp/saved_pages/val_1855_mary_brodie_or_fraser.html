The parishes available in each county changes, depending which year is chosen. This is because county and parish boundaries changed several times between 1855 and 1940.

Remember that surname spelling can vary.

Improve your searching by selecting surname search options, such as 'names that begin with' or 'fuzzy matching.'

For help in choosing the right surname option read our guidance on surnames.

Please note that in the valuation rolls, surnames beginning with Mc, Mac, M' and other variations, were not used consistently by Assessors in drawing up the rolls and most Mc/Mac/M' surnames have been standardised to Mac in the index data.

Search Options

Search by group for a company, for example, 'John Brown Ltd'; multiple tenants; public authorities; churches; multiples owners, for example, 'Mr and Mrs Smith'; trustees or heirs to individuals, for example, 'the heirs of the late Robert MacKay.'
 

Select a search option from 'tenants and occupiers', 'owners only' or 'all'.

Valuation rolls list the owners, tenants and occupiers of each property.

For guidance on owners, tenants and occupiers see our guide on valuation rolls.

How Place Name Links and Confidence Levels Work

The place of birth shown here is recorded exactly as it appears on the original vital statistics record. Original entries may include misspellings, abbreviations, general locations, or the recorder's interpretation. In rare cases, transcription or data entry errors may also exist.

The Provincial Archives uses an automated process to attempt to match each place name and provide a link to an official community in our Communities Database. This process is accurate in most cases, but matches are not guaranteed. A confidence indicator appears when a direct match is found.

👉🏻 No link is shown when the place name cannot be matched, when no place was recorded, when the name appears to be outside New Brunswick, or when it represents a county or parish rather than a community.

Confidence Colours
  • Green — Very high confidence in the match
  • Yellow — Medium-high confidence
  • Blue — Medium confidence
  • Purple — Multiple possible communities share this name

These indicators reflect how likely the link is to point to the correct official community.

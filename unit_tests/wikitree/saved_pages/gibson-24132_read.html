Biography

Mary Adeline Gibson was born in 1914; this is listed as 1915 on several census records. She is the daughter of William Gibson and Catherine Martin. In 1940, Mary was married to Herbert Webb and was living in Kansas City, Missouri. She is dicernable because she is the person listed as the respondent on the census, and she lists her birthplace as Cass Lake, MN, and Herbert as being born in Brainerd.

Mary Adeline Gibson may have become Mary Ada Boone before she married Herbert (callled "Bud").

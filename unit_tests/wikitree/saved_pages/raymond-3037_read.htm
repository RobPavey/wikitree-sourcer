    Biography

    Alice Alberta Raymond was born on 26 Nov 1910[1][2] to Nellie (Poirier) Raymond in St Francois County, Missouri, United States of America. Nellie identified Alice's biological father as James Albert Davis.[1]

    Alice (Raymond) Peterson was adopted 11 Apr 1911
    Location: Category:St. Louis, Missouri
    Adoptive Parents Biological Parents
    Father: Harry H Watson Father: James Albert Davis
    Mother: Sula (Coup) Watson Mother: Nellie (Poirier) Raymond

    Alice was surrendered by Nellie to the Children's Home Society of Missouri on 16 Dec 1910.[1] She was adopted as Alice Ursula Watson by Harry H Watson and Sula (Coup) Watson on 11 Apr 1911, 89 days after placement in their home.[1]

    Alice married Courtney Charles Alexander Peterson about 1925.[3][4]

    Alice lived in Maplewood[5][6][7][8][8][9][10][11][2] and the city of St. Louis[4], both in Missouri.

    Alice passed away at Deaconess Hospital in the city of St Louis, Missouri, United States of America on 28 May 1974[12][13] from kidney disease.[2] She was buried on 31 May 1974[2][12] at Laurel Hill Memorial Gardens in Pagedale, St Louis County, Missouri, United States of America.[14]

    Research Notes

    Alice's biological mother, Nellie Raymond, listed James Albert Daives [sic] as her biological father in the children's home surrender paperwork.[1] Both of Alice's parental relationships are supported by DNA evidence.

    The birth date on Alice's SSDI record was 26 Nov 1909.[13] But her adoption records and her death certificate both reflected 26 Nov 1910.[1][2] The latter was selected as her birth date since the information in the adoption records was recorded shortly after her birth.

    No marriage record has been found for Alice and Courtney. The 1930 US Census indicates that Courtney was first married at age 23 and Alice was first married at age 16.[4] Courtney reached 23 years of age on 23 Sep 1922. Alice reached 16 years of age on 26 Nov 1926. There are no known prior spouses for either of them. When her adoptive mother Sula died on 23 Jul 1926, Alice was identified in her obituary as Mrs. Courtney Peterson.[3] Courtney Lee Peterson was the first known child to be born to Courtney and Alice on 21 Jan 1926.[15] Their marriage year has been estimated at 1925.
